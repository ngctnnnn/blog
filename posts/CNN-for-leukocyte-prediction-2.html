4. Edge detection using Canny method #

This method uses two threshold levels high and low. We will first use the high threshold to find the starting point of the edge, then determine the path direction of the border based on consecutive pixels that have a value greater than the low threshold. Only remove points with values less than the low threshold. Small (hard to see) borders will be selected if they are associated with large (easy to see) borders.
